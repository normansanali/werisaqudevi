Lego Marvel minifigures series 2 release date and character list

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  296 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

LEGO signifies a multitude of things. For some people, it may be those annoying little bricks that everyone will step on at some point. For others, LEGO really represents the ginormous diversity of collectibles that one can build up from scratch. From Star Wars to even K-Pop groups, LEGO has provided fans of these little brick blocks a chance to get figurines of their favorite series, musicians, and films, and even the chance to get entire worlds built in blocks.

In 2021, the brand decided to expand its influence through the Marvel stratosphere, revealing its exclusive 71031 Marvel Collectible minifigures series. Comprised of characters like Wanda Maximoff, Loki, and Bucky Barnes, the collection ran through the major series that were available in Disney Plus at the time. Gifting fans once again, LEGO is releasing a whole new set, made up of new characters.

When are the Marvel LEGO minifigures releasing and who are the characters?

Releasing in cardboard packaging on September, 1, 2023, the LEGO 71039 Marvel Studios Minifigures Series 2 will follow up the two existing sets that include Marvel characters, namely the 71031 set, and LEGO Minifigures 71038 Disney 100 that was launched in May. It will feature 12 new figures, and you can collect any of the following:

  • Moon Knight
  • Kate Bishop
  • Hawkeye
  • Mr. Knight
  • She-Hulk
  • Goliath
  • Beast
  • Echo
  • Wolverine
  • Storm
  • Werewolf By Night
  • Agatha

So far, the company has yet to confirm the prices of the units, but unless an increase is announced, the price of each LEGO figure should be around $4.99/€3,99 each, much like the previous collections. Although there is still a few months left, these new collectibles are already receiving quite a demand from fans, and in September it will all be available to the public.
