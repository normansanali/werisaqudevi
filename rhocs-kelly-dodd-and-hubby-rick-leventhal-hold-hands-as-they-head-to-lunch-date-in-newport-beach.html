RHOC's Kelly Dodd and hubby Rick Leventhal hold hands as they head to lunch date in Newport Beach

 Posted on September 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  501 words  |  Martina Birk

They have made their base on the West Coast after tying the knot this past October.

And on Thursday, Kelly Dodd and Rick Leventhal were snapped heading to lunch in the upscale Southern California community of Newport Beach.

The Real Housewives Of Orange County star, 45, and the Fox News correspondent, 61, held hands as they headed to the eatery after arriving in their sporty black coupe. 

Date: Real Housewives of Orange County star Kelly Dodd and Fox News correspondent Rick Leventhal held hands as they headed to lunch in Newport Beach, California, on Thursday

The couple were maskless as they exited their vehicle and crossed the parking lot.

Once inside, however, they donned face coverings.

Dodd, who’s made controversial comments about the COVID-19 pandemic, donned a hot pink face covering with the sassy motto ‘In Your Face’ written across it in capital letters.

On February 1, she was dropped from her partnership with Positive Beverages due to her ‘controversial views and opinions’ on the coronavirus after dining maskless with pals in Newport Beach and toasting to being ‘super spreaders’.   

SoCal living: The Bravo reality star, 45, and the Fox News correspondent, 61, tied the knot this past October and have made the West Coast their base

Message: Inside the eatery, Dodd, who’s made controversial comments about the COVID-19 pandemic, donned a hot pink face covering with the sassy motto ‘In Your Face’ written across it

The couple met at a barbecue at Leventhal’s house in the Hamptons on July 6, 2019, after being introduced by Real Housewives of New York star Ramona Singer. 

Since his marriage, however, Leventhal has commited to relocating to the West Coast.

In November, he sold his three-bedroom New York pad for $750,000 and just last week, he listed his Hamptons home for sale with an asking price of $1.395 million.

No longer needed: The couple met at a barbecue at Leventhal’s house in the Hamptons on July 6, 2019. Last week, Leventhal listed the home for sale for $1.395 million

Letting go of his past: Leventhal, in a statement, said he had owned the home for 25 years but the couple’s decision to be based on the West Coast led to him to part with it 

The residence with four bedrooms, and two bathrooms is located in Westhampton, Long Island. 

Leventhal, in a statement, said he had owned the home for 25 years but the couple’s decision to be based on the West Coast led to him to part with it.

‘This is where Kelly and I met, so it will always hold a special place in our hearts, but we are on the West Coast now,’ he told the NY Post.

‘One day, if and when we move back East, we hope to buy or build another home for ourselves in the Hamptons.’

Wedded bliss: It’s the third marriage for Dodd, who shares a 14-year-old daughter Jolie with ex Michael Dodd, and the second marriage for Leventhal (pictured in December 2019)
