Yuya (YouTuber) – Professional Life

She was a winner of a makeup contest in the year 2009. The interesting fact is that she was only 16 years old at that time. Soon after, she joined YouTube and began her career as a YouTuber. She named her channel “lady16makeup” in the beginning but now it has been changed to “Yuya”. She started posting various kinds of makeup videos. And surprisingly, her videos earned millions of views within a very short period. She drew much attention to the displays thanks to her unusual voice.

She was one of the seven female Youtube personalities who were a part of the ‘United Nations Sustainable Development Action Campaign’, in the year 2016. The campaign’s goal was to promote gender equality and empower women and girls around the world. Moreover, she has written and published two books as well.
