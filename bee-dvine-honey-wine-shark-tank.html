Bee D’Vine is a brand of sustainably-produced honey wine. Crafted and barrel-aged in Sonoma Valley, California, this wine stands out for its exceptional quality. The Honey Wine Company, the maker of Bee D’Vine, offers two delightful varieties of honey wine – the dry Brut and the semi-sweet Demi Sec. Each bottle of Bee D’Vine is aged for at least 15 months to ensure a remarkable flavor profile.

A portion of Bee D’Vine sales goes towards supporting and helping the Kafa community transition to modern beekeeping methods. By doing so, they not only promote sustainable practices but also generate income for the community while conserving the beautiful Kafa rainforest.

The Inspiration Behind Bee D’Vine

Ayele Solomon, an entrepreneur and conservationist, found his inspiration to create Bee D’Vine from his passion for saving bees and a traditional Ethiopian honey wine called t’ej. During his visits to Ethiopia, Solomon marveled at the beauty of the remaining Kafa rainforests and the vital role bees played in their preservation. However, he was saddened by the threat of deforestation and the decline of bee populations.

Solomon’s encounter with t’ej, a honey wine unique to the region, sparked his imagination and fueled his determination to make a positive impact. He recognized the cultural significance of honey wine in Ethiopia and saw an opportunity to use this age-old tradition as a means to save bees and conserve the Kafa rainforest.

A true conservationist at heart, Solomon embarked on a mission to create Bee D’Vine, a wine brand that not only delivers exquisite taste but also supports environmental preservation and empowers local communities.

By using locally sourced honey from Sonoma and innovative winemaking techniques, Bee D’Vine celebrates the deep connection between nature and flavor, showcasing the unique characteristics of Ethiopian honey. Each bottle of Bee D’Vine honey wine encapsulates Ayele Solomon’s dedication to bee conservation and his commitment to sustainable practices.

The Unique Process of Honey Wine

What sets Bee D’Vine honey wine apart is its unique production process. Unlike traditional grape wines, Bee D’Vine is made using only two ingredients: locally sourced honey from Sonoma and sparkling water. This minimalist approach allows the natural flavors of the honey to shine through, resulting in a tannin-free wine with a clean finish.

The production process begins by selecting the finest local honey, ensuring the highest quality and flavor. This honey is then carefully fermented and blended with sparkling water, creating a delicate and refreshing wine. The wine is aged for a minimum of 15 months, allowing it to develop depth and complexity.

The end result is a honey wine that showcases the true essence of the Sonoma Valley, with subtle sweetness and a crisp, clean finish. Bee D’Vine’s commitment to using local ingredients and their meticulous production process ensures that each bottle delivers a truly exceptional tasting experience.

Emphasizing Quality with Local Ingredients

Bee D’Vine takes great pride in sourcing their honey locally from Sonoma. By supporting local beekeepers, they not only contribute to the community but also guarantee the freshness and authenticity of their honey wine. This emphasis on local ingredients adds a unique regional character to each bottle, creating a truly distinctive product.

The use of sparkling water instead of traditional grape juice further sets Bee D’Vine apart in the world of wine production. The delicate effervescence adds a refreshing quality to the wine, enhancing its overall appeal. This innovative approach to honey wine production results in a beverage that is light, vibrant, and perfect for any occasion.

The Impact on the Kafa Community

Bee D’Vine is committed to making a positive impact on the Kafa community in Ethiopia. The company recognizes the importance of supporting local communities and sustainable practices in the production of honey wine. By working closely with the Kafa community, Bee D’Vine aims to create a win-win situation that benefits both the environment and the community.

One of the key initiatives undertaken by Bee D’Vine is to help the Kafa community transition to modern beekeeping methods. This transition is expected to have multiple positive outcomes. Firstly, it will result in increased honey production, allowing the community to meet the growing demand for their products. Secondly, it will generate more income for the community, leading to improved standards of living and greater economic stability.

In addition to boosting honey production and income generation, this transition to modern beekeeping methods also contributes to the conservation of the Kafa rainforest. By adopting sustainable practices, the Kafa community can protect their natural environment and preserve biodiversity. This is crucial for maintaining the delicate balance of the ecosystem and ensuring the long-term viability of honey production.

Bee D’Vine contributes a portion of its sales to the Kafa community, providing them with the resources and support needed to transition to modern beekeeping methods. This financial assistance helps cover the costs of training, equipment, and infrastructure required for sustainable honey production. By investing in the future of the Kafa community, Bee D’Vine is fostering a stronger and more resilient local economy while promoting environmental conservation.

Through this partnership, Bee D’Vine not only ensures the availability of high-quality honey for its wine production but also helps create a positive ripple effect within the community. The increased income generated from honey production enables the Kafa community to invest in education, healthcare, and other essential services, further improving their quality of life.

By supporting local beekeepers and promoting modern beekeeping methods, Bee D’Vine is not only making a difference in the lives of the Kafa community but also demonstrating the power of sustainable business practices. The company’s commitment to social and environmental responsibility sets a commendable example for the industry, showcasing how businesses can create positive change while producing exceptional products.

Ayele Solomon’s Journey on Shark Tank

Ayele Solomon, the founder of Bee D’Vine, had an incredible journey on Shark Tank, a popular television show known for launching successful businesses. In his investment pitch, Solomon aimed to secure $750,000 in exchange for a 20% equity stake in his company. With confidence and conviction, he showcased his unique honey wine and shared his unwavering commitment to bee conservation.

The Sharks were captivated by Solomon’s pitch and recognized the potential in Bee D’Vine. Lori Greiner, Robert Herjavec, Daniel Lubetzky, and Mark Cuban saw an opportunity to invest in a brand with a distinct product and a meaningful mission. Together, they made a combined offer of $750,000 for a 40% equity stake in the company.

Solomon accepted the offer, striking a deal with all four Sharks. The partnership between Bee D’Vine and the Sharks signifies not only a significant financial boost but also an endorsement of the brand’s vision and potential for growth.
