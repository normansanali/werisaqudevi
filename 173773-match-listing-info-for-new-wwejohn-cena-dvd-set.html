Match Listing &amp; Info For New WWE/John Cena DVD Set

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  448 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Brian S. sent along the following:

I was on Amazon and i was looking over some release dates on some WWE stuff and I came across a John Cena highlight film called: “WWE: The John Cena Experience” and this title will be released on November 16, 2010. Tthe price they have for it is 31.49.

Here is the ist of the matches that they have on the DVD:

1. John Cena vs. Big Show – WrestleMania 20 (first WrestleMania match and first title win)

2. John Cena vs. Kurt Angle – No Way Out 2005 (shows his true potential as a main eventer)

3. John Cena vs. JBL – WrestleMania 21 (wins his first WWE title and become “The Man”)

4. John Cena vs. Chris Jericho – SummerSlam 2005 (first major title defense as part of Monday Night RAW)

5. John Cena vs. Triple H – WrestleMania 22 (shocks the world and proves he is the “real deal”)

6. John Cena vs. Edge (Steel Cage) – RAW 2006 (great match that ended the best rivalry in 2006)

7. John Cena vs. Umaga (Last Man Standing) – Royal Rumble 2007 (one of Cena’s greatest matches and shows how he never gives up)

8. John Cena vs. Shawn Michaels – WrestleMania 23 (another great title defense cementing himself as the best of the best)

9. John Cena vs. The Great Khali – Judgment Day 2007 (pulls out a great match with Khali with a shocker of an ending)

10. John Cena vs. Umaga vs. The Great Khali – RAW 2007 (defends his title against the two biggest monsters in WWE)

11. John Cena vs. Bobby Lashley – Great American Bash 2007 (two great Americans wrestle an exciting match)

12. John Cena vs. Randy Orton – SummerSlam 2007 (first title match of an intense rivalry)

13. John Cena vs. JBL (Parking Lot Brawl) – Great American Bash 2008 (one of the most brutal brawls in his career)

14. John Cena vs. Batista – SummerSlam 2008 (the two biggest stars since the Attitude Era collide for the first time)

15. John Cena vs. Chris Jericho – Armageddon 2008 (first World Heavyweight title defense against an old foe)

16. John Cena vs. Edge (Last Man Standing) – Backlash 2009 (renewal of a legendary rivalry)

17. John Cena vs. Randy Orton vs. Triple H – Night of Champions 2009 (rematch from WrestleMania 24)

18. John Cena vs. Randy Orton (Iron Man Match) – Bragging Rights 2009 (one of the best matches in 2009)

19. John Cena vs. Triple H vs. Shawn Michaels – Survivor Series 2009 (defends against D-Generation X)

20. John Cena vs. Batista – WrestleMania 26 (epic rematch worthy of WrestleMania)
