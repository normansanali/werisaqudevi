Who to Look for in 2023 Bowman Baseball?

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  867 words  |  Martina Birk

The Bowman 2023 collection is highly anticipated by baseball card collectors and enthusiasts alike. It offers the opportunity to acquire cards featuring some of the most promising talent in the world of baseball.

In this comprehensive guide, we will take a look into the potential impact players to look for in Bowman 2023, providing detailed insights into their backgrounds, skills, achievements, and reasons why they should be closely watched.

Paul Skenes - Pittsburgh Pirates

Background

Paul Skenes, a towering 6-foot-6, 250-pound pitcher, was the No. 1 overall pick in the 2023 draft. Known for his triple-digit fastball, wipeout slider, and underrated changeup, Skenes is expected to make a swift ascent to the big leagues.

Skills and Potential

Skenes possesses a rare combination of size, power, and agility. With the potential to become a perennial All-Star, his ability to fill up the strike zone sets him apart as an electrifying collegiate arm with immense potential.

Max Clark - Detroit Tigers

Background

Max Clark, an explosive outfielder with elite defensive potential, is known for his double-plus running ability and rocket arm. His impressive bat speed and sweet left-handed swing make him a dynamic player to watch.

Skills and Potential

Clark's five-tool potential, coupled with his defensive prowess and promising batting abilities, position him as a player with massive upside and the capability to impact the game in multiple ways.

Jacob Wilson - Oakland Athletics

Background

Jacob Wilson, the son of former All-Star shortstop Jack Wilson, boasts one of the best hit tools in his class. His standout performance at Grand Canyon University and with USA Baseball's Collegiate National Team in 2022 has garnered attention.

Skills and Potential

Wilson's smooth defensive skills at shortstop, strong throwing arm, and exceptional hit tool make him a standout prospect with the potential to be a top-of-the-order everyday shortstop and a contender for batting titles.

Blake Mitchell - Kansas City Royals

Background

Regarded as the top prep catcher in the draft, Blake Mitchell's transition to focusing on catching has showcased his strong defensive capabilities. 

Skills and Potential

Mitchell's tools as a strong defensive catcher, combined with his power potential from the left side of the plate, make him a player to watch for impact both behind and at the plate.

Wyatt Langford - Texas Rangers

Background

Wyatt Langford, built like a linebacker, possesses a rare blend of plus power and speed. His impressive performance at the University of Florida and in the professional leagues highlights his potential as a standout prospect.

Skills and Potential

Langford's blend of power, speed, contact ability, and patience at the plate make him an exciting prospect with the potential for 30/30 upside and strong on-base skills.

Tommy Troy - Arizona Diamondbacks

Background

Tommy Troy's top flight contact rates at Stanford, combined with his above-average power and good speed, have solidified his position as a promising prospect. 

Skills and Potential

Troy's impressive contact rates, power, and speed, along with his solid average, position him as a prospect with the potential for 20/20 production and a valuable skillset for the Diamondbacks.

Chase Davis - St. Louis Cardinals

Background

Chase Davis, with a silky smooth left-handed swing reminiscent of Carlos Gonzalez, made a significant impact in his Junior season at the University of Arizona. His prowess at the plate has garnered attention for his potential as a middle-of-the-order masher.

Skills and Potential

Davis's ability to consistently generate big exit velocities, low chase rates, and impressive power potential positions him as a player with the potential to be a significant offensive force and an on-base machine.

Jacob Gonzalez - Chicago White Sox

Background

Jacob Gonzalez, a hit machine during his time at Ole Miss, possesses an excellent feel for the strike zone and strong baseball instincts.

Skills and Potential

Gonzalez's hitting ability, combined with his strong overall instincts and excellent strike zone discipline, position him as a player with the potential to consistently produce and contribute to the White Sox lineup.

Bryce Eldridge - San Francisco Giants

Background

Bryce Eldridge presents a strong case for blossoming into a legitimate two-way player, standing at 6-foot-7 with exceptional control both on the mound and in the batter's box. 

Skills and Potential

Eldridge's easy plus power potential, remarkable control, and impressive skills as both a hitter and a pitcher position him as a compelling prospect with the potential to make a significant impact for the Giants.

Colt Emerson - Seattle Mariners

Background

Colt Emerson, a polished teenage bat in the Minor Leagues, has seamlessly transitioned into professional baseball, displaying above-average power and impressive hitting abilities. 

Skills and Potential

Emerson's impressive power, ability to make throws from all angles, and polished bat in the Minor Leagues position him as an impactful prospect with the potential to contribute significantly to the Mariners' lineup.

 

Overall, the Bowman 2023 collection presents an exciting opportunity to acquire cards featuring some of the most promising talent in baseball.

From electrifying pitchers to explosive outfielders, the prospects featured in this collection offer a glimpse into the future of the sport.

With their unique skills, potential, and promising backgrounds, these individuals are poised to make a significant impact in Bowman 2023 and beyond, making them players to watch closely as their careers unfold.
