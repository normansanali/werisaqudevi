Nicholas Lou Saban Jr. is an American football coach who has been the head football coach at the University of Alabama since 2007. Saban was born on October 31, 1951, in Fairmont, West Virginia.

Nick Saban married his wife Terry in 1971. The couple has two adopted children: Nicholas and Kristen.
