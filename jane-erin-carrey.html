Jane Erin Carrey

Jane Erin Carrey is well-known as the only daughter of a famous actor and comedian, Jim Carrey. She is a singer, songwriter, actor, and producer who first caught the media attention after auditioning on the 11th season of “American Idol”. Getting eliminated although came as a huge setback, she chose to pursue her musical passion. She then became the frontwoman for her own band “The Jane Carrey Band”.

In 2017, she collaborated with her father on his biopic film “Jim Carrey: I Needed Color” as a playback singer. Moreover, two of her songs were used in her dad’s movie “Dumb and Dumber To“. Living under the shadow of a comedic genius may have made it difficult for Jane to carve her individual identity but she always had full-support from her father in whatever she did.
