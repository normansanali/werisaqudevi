See Megan Fox Debut Icy Platinum Blonde Hair

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  292 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Megan Fox has gone blonde.

The Jennifer's Body actor revealed her new hair color on Instagram yesterday. In the pic, she wears her new platinum blonde hair, so light it's nearly silver, in a wavy blowout with a middle part showing dark brown roots. She also shared that the new hair is for her upcoming heist film Johnny and Clyde.

"This is what the devil’s daughter looks like. #JohnnyAndClyde Coming Spring 2022," she captioned the pic.

In the new film, an update on the Bonnie and Clyde story, Fox plays a casino owner and crime boss targeted by "two eponymous serial killers who are madly in love and on an endless crime spree," per Deadline.

Fox's hairstylist Miles Jeffries also shared the new look on his own Instagram, writing in the caption, "Major Hair transformation for @meganfox." He also tagged artisan wigmaker Merria Dearman, so Fox may have chosen to rock a wig rather than dye her own brunette locks.

The Transformers actor had a series of stunning fashion moments last month. She walked the VMAs red carpet alongside her boyfriend Machine Gun Kelly in a sultry naked look, wearing a sheer Mugler gown with sparkling embroidery with gold pumps and a wet hairstyle.

The next day, she attended her first Met Gala in a fiery red beaded gown by Dundas, featuring cutouts with criss cross laces and a dramatic thigh-high slit. She also wore red Jimmy Choo platforms and Lorraine Schwartz jewelry, and added blunt bangs to her slicked-back ponytail.
