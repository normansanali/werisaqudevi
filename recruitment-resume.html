Don't let your athletic background go unnoticed in your future career endeavors. The skills you develop as a student-athlete are highly valued by employers, such as communication, time management, self-motivation, and goal orientation. Including these transferable skills on your resume will not only impress college coaches but also future employers. It's essential to discuss these skills during your career development process and leverage them in job searches.

When describing your athletic experience on your resume, treat it like any other campus involvement or work experience. Highlight your role on the team, how you were perceived by coaches and teammates, and your notable accomplishments. Emphasize skills that are transferable to your desired career and demonstrate the qualifications required for the position you're applying for. By showcasing the skills you gained from managing your sporting commitments in a professional manner, you become an attractive candidate to potential employers.
