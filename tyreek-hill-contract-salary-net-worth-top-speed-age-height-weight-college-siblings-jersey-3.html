Tyreek Hill is an American football wide receiver for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League.

Tyreek Hill was born on March 1, 1994, in Pearson, Georgia, United States. He was raised by his mother Virginia Hill.

Tyreek Hill attended Coffee High School, where he won both the 100 meters and 200 meters at the 2012 Georgia 5A state meet. He later attended Garden City Community College, Oklahoma State University, and the University of West Alabama.

Tyreek Hill was a USA Today All-American track and field selection in 2012. He was selected by the Kansas City Chiefs in the fifth round (165th overall) of the 2016 NFL Draft and was the first player from West Alabama to be drafted since 1974.
