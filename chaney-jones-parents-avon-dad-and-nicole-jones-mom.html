Meet Chaney Jones Dad, Avon Jones

Avon Jones is the father of Chaney Jones.

  • Avon Jones Age

Reportedly born in 1969, Avon Timothy Jones is 52 years old as of 2022.

  • Avon Jones Job

By profession, Avon Jones is a therapist and CEO of First State Behavioral Health. He is a 2001 graduate of Lincoln University and holds three different degrees Ms, MEd, and NCC. His main area of practice is Impulse Control Disorders, Mood Disorders, Psychosis, and Thinking Disorders.

He deals with clients having issues with depression, anxiety, abuse, anger management, trauma, parenting assistance, divorce, or substance abuse. According to Psychology Today, the counseling sessions with him can cost anywhere from $75-$150. With command in English & Spanish language, Avon applies treatment approaches such as Cognitive Behavioral, EMDR, Intervention, and Solution Focused Brief to deal with his clients’ issues.

Meet Chaney Jones Mom, Nicole Jones

Coming to the Matriarch, Chaney Jones’s mom is Nicole Councell Jones.

  • Nicole Jones Age

Born in 1973, Nicole Jones is 48 years old (as of 2022).

  • Nicole Jones Job

Nicole Jones is currently working as the principal of Dover Air Force Base Middle School (DABM). Her appointment as the principal of the school was announced in February 2017. She officially took the office on 16 February 2017 after former principal Dr. David Santore retired from his job.

Before DABM, Nicole was working in Nellie H. Stokes Elementary School, also in the Caesar Rodney School District where she was working since 2012. Before becoming an administrator, she served as a 5th-grade teacher, academic liaison teacher (ALT), and as assistant principal. Before coming to Caesar Rodney, she was a middle school mathematics teacher.

Under her leadership, Stokes school has been the recipient of four State Recognition Awards from 2012 to 2017. Before starting her job, Nicole said, “It is my distinct honor and privilege to serve as the next principal of Dover Air Base Middle. I am looking forward to working alongside the amazing staff and school community. It is my goal to continue the tradition of excellence to which the community has grown accustomed. I truly believe you always have to finish stronger than where you start and I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead.”
