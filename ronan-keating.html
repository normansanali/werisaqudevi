Birth Name: Ronan Patrick John Keating

Place of Birth: Dublin, Ireland

Date of Birth: 3 March, 1977

Ethnicity: Irish

Ronan Keating is an Irish singer, songwriter, musician, philanthropist, and television and radio presenter.

He is the son of Marie (Dempsey), a mobile hairdresser, and Gerry Keating, a lorry driver. Ronan is married to Australian fashion designer and brand ambassador Storm Keating. He has three children with his former wife Yvonne Connolly; and two children with Storm. His nephew is professional footballer Ruairí Keating.

Ronan’s paternal grandfather was named Mick Keating.

Ronan’s paternal grandmother was named Annie Murray.
