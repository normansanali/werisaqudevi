Daniel Day-Lewis Awards and Nominations, Net Worth, Wife and Family

 Posted on September 29, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  855 words  |  Martina Birk

The life of a movie star would be eventful. One movie is done and another is ready to go due to the stereotype that an artist has to star in as many movies as possible in order to win many prestigious awards as an actor. Nevertheless, one of the most selective actors in the film industry, Daniel Day-Lewis refutes this hypothesis.

Despite starring in only six films since 1998 with up to five years between roles, he managed to establish himself as the only male actor in history to win three awards in the lead actor category and the one of only three male actors to win three Oscars. He has also won four BAFTA Awards for Best Actor, three Screen Actors Guild Awards and two Golden Globe Awards.

Wife and family of Daniel Day-Lewis

Day-Lewis is taciturn with information about his personal life, he can be overprotective of his family and his life. But, pieces of his private life can be found online. He had an affair with Isabella Adjani, a French actress for six years. The relationship resulted in the birth of their son Gabriel-Kane Day-Lewis, born in 1995 in New York.

He then met and married Rebecca Miller, the daughter of The Crucible playwright Arthur Miller and their marriage is blessed with two sons, Ronan Cal Day-Lewis (b. 1998) and Cashel Blake Day-Lewis (b. 2002 )

Awards and nominations

Day-Lewis is considered a method actor despite his years at the Bristol Old Vic where he learned the traditional way of acting. He is known for his constant dedication and research on his roles. He prefers to fully maintain his character throughout the filming period of his films, even at the expense of his health, which has earned him countless awards in the film industry.

He won three Best Actor Oscars for his performance in My Left Foot (1989), There Will Be Blood (2007) and Lincoln (2012). In the same category, he was also nominated for In the Name of the Father (1993), Gangs of New York (2002) and Phantom Thread (2017). In addition to the above, he has won four Best Actor BAFTA Awards, three Screen Actors Guild Awards and two Golden Globe Awards. To top it all off, Time Magazine named Daniel Day-Lewis the world’s greatest actor in November 2012.
