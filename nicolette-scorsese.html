Career

She started her career as a photo model. She made her television debut as an actress with the series ‘The A-Team’ (1985) as ‘Cindy.’

She has also acted in the American TV series, Charles in Charge (1987), L.A. Law (1994), Rebel Highway (1994), and NYPD Blue (1995-2000). She made her film debut with ‘Perfect Victims’ (1988) as ‘Melissa Cody.’

She has acted in the American films, National Lampoon’s Christmas Vacation (1989), Aspen Extreme (1993), Boxing Helena (1993), Girls in Prison (1994), and The Ultimate Law (1996). She was last seen in the series ‘NYPD Blue’ in 2000 in the role of ‘Francine.’
