Not only are Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift the subject of various engagement rumors, but now they’re getting offers to help make a proposal happen.

Speaking to Page Six, Philadelphia jewelry designer Steven Singe said he wants to offer Travis a $1 million custom engagement ring he can use to propose to Taylor.

The Jeweler Is A Taylor Swift Fan Who's Hoping For Tickets

Singe revealed that his daughter is a Swiftie, which has motivated him to reach out to Taylor and Travis publicly with the unique offer.

Taylor Swift has been happily dating Travis Kelce for the last several weeks, but before him she had plenty of older boyfriends.

After debuting their relationship last year, Taylor and Travis came one of the most talked about couples in Hollywood and have now been dodging engagement rumors for months. Some reports claimed Travis planned to propose over the holidays, while others said he’d do it for Taylor’s birthday, though both occasions came without an engagement.

More recently, Page Six released a report claiming the couple plan to get engaged this summer, around the time they’ll make a year together. “Taylor and Travis discussed it and there is a plan,” a source said. “They will get engaged on their one-year anniversary in July.”

Travis And Taylor's Relationship Has Faced Controversy

Despite being lovey-dovey in various public appearances, Taylor and Travis’ relationship hasn’t been without controversy. His past comments have gotten him in trouble, including when he said he wanted to find a “breeder” to have kids with only to appear his mother – remarks he made shortly before getting together with Taylor.

His mom even got him in hot water when she revealed that Travis had a bad habit of yelling at her during the holidays for having no cousins to play with, despite that being out of her control.

Perhaps even more telling, Taylor received a public warning from Travis’ ex-girlfriend Maya Benberry, who said he has a history of cheating and that the singer should be wary of his intentions.

“The man that I knew him to be when we dated,” she said last year. “Only time will tell but like the saying always goes, once a cheater, always a cheater.”

“I’m sure by now she has mastered the ability to see who is really there for her - and who is just using her,” Maya continued. “I can only hope that she comes out the teacher in this situation and not the student.”
