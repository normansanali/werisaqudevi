Who is Rudy Gobert?

Rudy Gobert was born on June 26, 1992 in Saint-Quentin, France. He is 30 years old and stands at a height of 7 ft 1 in (2.16 m). 

His father, Rudy Bourgarel,  played college basketball in the United States for the Marist Red Foxes from 1985 to 1988 and that already is a signal that Rudy inherited his basketball skills from his father. 

Rudy Gobert went from playing basketball as a kid to becoming one of the best in the game currently.
