Who is Jesse Metcalfe dating following split with fiancée Cara Santana? The 'Desperate Housewives' star has reportedly moved on. Details inside.

Earlier this year, Jesse Metcalfe and fiancée Cara Santana called it quits on their 13-year romance after the actor was photographed getting cozy with two women. At the time, a source told E! News that the actress was "heartbroken" over the revelation and "completely blindsided by the photos of him with other women."

Though their split was seemingly not amicable, the two have reportedly since moved on following their three-year engagement. So, who is the John Tucker Must Die actor dating? Keep reading to find out more about his current relationship status.

Who is Jesse Metcalfe dating following his split with Cara Santana?

Less than a year after his breakup with his longtime girlfriend, Jesse has reportedly moved on with Canadian model Corin Jamie Lee Clark. "They feel like they've been together forever even though it's only been a few months. He's crazy about her," a source told E! News about the new romance. "Jesse has gotten serious with Corin very quickly. He thinks she's amazing and they have been inseparable." 

The outlet reported that the pair has been quarantining together in both Los Angeles and Canada. At this time, the reported couple have not made their romance social media official. But, it's definitely only a matter of time...

And, Jesse is not the only one to have found another partner following the messy split. Cara was recently spotted with 30 Seconds to Mars drummer Shannon Leto. 

Cara Santana was spotted with Jared Leto's older brother.

The Vida actress was photographed packing on the PDA (in pics obtained by The Dailymail) while on a beach date in California. "Cara has healed from the heartbreak and has moved on. She's truly in a good place," a source revealed to E! News. Multiple outlets reported that the two are in a relationship. 

Back in April, rumors started to circulate that Cara and Jesse were possibly trying to reconcile their failed relationship after being spotted together running errands. At the time, an insider told Us Weekly, “They are definitely not back together. They own a home together. … Jesse lives in the guest house." Adding, "They had to make the mature decision to deal with this situation together like many people going through a breakup right now.” 

The following month, the actress/blogger opened up about her mental health and reflected on her happiness. “Given that the total landscape of our world has changed, it’s been a really interesting time to reflect and reconnect,” she told Retreat magazine back in May. 

She continued, “I feel at times that we’ve lost our moral compass. We’ve disconnected from our values, we’re destroying the plant, and literally, this pandemic has caused everyone to stop. I feel like we can either come out of this the same or we can come out of it differently, in a good way. This is a time for everyone to self-reflect.”
