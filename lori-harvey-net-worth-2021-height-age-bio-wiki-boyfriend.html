Lori Harvey Early Life

Lori was born on January 13, 1997, in Memphis, Tennessee, but grew up in Atlanta, Georgia. When she was young, she was a very talented Equestrian. She ended up competing and winning several competitions. She had an unfortunate accident while riding her horse in 2015.

Harvey’s Equestrian career ended after the incident. She then pursued a career in modeling and has since signed contracts with LA Models in the US and Select Model Management in Europe.

Moving on to her family, Lori is the step-daughter of the famous host Steve Harvey while Donnell Woods is her biological father. She has two biological brothers named Jason and Morgan. Likewise, she also have two half-sisters Brandi and Karli, and two half-brothers named Brodrick Harvey Jr. and Wynton.

Lori Harvey Net Worth & Career

Lori Harvey appeared in several advertisements as a child model before discovering her love of horseback riding. Lori became so skilled as an Equestrian that she competed in the Olympics. Unfortunately, Lori suffered a back injury during a competition in Lexington, Kentucky that ended her career.

Lori switched to modeling career to deal with the disappointment of not being able to chase her dreams of being an Equestrian. She made her modeling debut in 2017 by working for Dolce & Gabbana. She has also walked the runway during fashion week in Paris and Milan.
