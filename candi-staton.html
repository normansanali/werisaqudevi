Biography

Candi Staton was born in Hanceville, AL on March 13, 1940. Alabama-born gospel and soul singer. Her hit songs include “Young Hearts Run Free” and “You Got the Love.” Her albums include Chance, Love Lifted Me, and The Anointing. She recorded a popular 1970 cover of Tammy Wynette‘s song “Stand By Your Man.” During her high school years in Nashville, Tennessee, she became a founding member of the Jewell Gospel Trio.
